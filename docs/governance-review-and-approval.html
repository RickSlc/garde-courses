Chapter 2 Governance Review and Approval

Most healthcare systems (HCS) have a governance committee that reviews proposals for implementation of information technology (IT) tools. Undergoing governance review and obtaining approval is a critical early step to take once you have decided to implement GARDE. The governance review and approval processes vary across institutions, but the overarching goals of governance reviews are typically to:

  1. prioritize implementation/customization of EHR functionality

  2. assess the potential impact of new/customized functionality on existing clinical information systems, clinicians and patients

  3. assess the effort needed to implement/customize the EHR functionality

  4. ensure there are robust channels for user feedback and the dissemination of systems-related information to end users
